Welcome,

I am an enthusiastic, motivated and happy-go-lucky web developer, loving life in Wellington, NZ.

I first discovered my passion for coding in October 2015 whilst part of a startup business accelerator program in Christchurch. Prior to this my occupation was managing bars, restaurants and hotels, which has provided me with advanced communication and organisational skills, an adept solution-orientated mentality and a customer centric attitude.

Over the last 18 months, I graduated Dev Academy, Wellington, as well as completed a plethora of online tutorials and am currently working as a web developer for Hospo360. I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3 & Javascript (ES6, AngularJS, VueJS & React), plus a range of testing and templating languages & frameworks (Cucumber, Gherkin, Tape). I have some experience working with back-end languages PHP & SQL and this is definitely an area where I am interested in upskilling in the future. My goal going forward is simply to grow bigger, better, faster, stronger through collaborative projects, work-based learning and self-directed study.

In my spare time you will find me either coding, cooking, traveling or enjoying the great outdoors.
